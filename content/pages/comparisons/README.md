# Comparison Pages

This folder contains comparison and buying-decision content for PloverCRM.

## Strategy

Use a comparison hub, pillar comparison pages, and a controlled competitor comparison cluster.

The goal is not to mass-publish thin “PloverCRM vs X” pages. The goal is to create useful SEO/AEO-friendly pages that help WordPress businesses choose the right tool for lead control after capture.

Comparison content should position PloverCRM as a focused WordPress-first lead CRM for businesses that want to manage captured leads, source context, reminders, follow-up, history, mobile access, and outcomes without adopting a broad external SaaS CRM.

## Editorial rules

- Stay WordPress-first.
- Sell lead control after capture, not generic CRM software.
- Be fair about when another tool is the better fit.
- Do not claim PloverCRM is better than large CRMs for every customer.
- Focus on lead source, owner, history, reminder, stage, next action, mobile access, and outcome visibility.
- Include native mobile follow-up as a major comparison dimension where relevant.
- Use comparison tables only when they improve decision clarity.
- Avoid unsupported pricing, feature, or performance claims.
- Verify competitor features, pricing, and mobile access before publishing final copy.
- Add a “Last reviewed” date only when a page has actually been checked.
