Help a buyer understand whether PloverCRM or the competitor is a better fit for their workflow.

## Editorial rules

- At least 40–60% of the page must be specific to the competitor.
- Be honest about where the competitor is stronger.
- Do not claim PloverCRM is better for every customer.
- Avoid exact pricing unless researched, dated, and verified.
- Include PloverCRM's real differentiators: WordPress-first lead capture, source tracking, reminders, stages, history, mobile follow-up, and outcomes.
- Add a short source/research note before publication.
