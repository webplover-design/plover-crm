## Strategic job

Clarify the difference between managing new leads after capture and running WordPress email marketing automation.

## Core angle

FluentCRM is strong for WordPress email marketing, contact segmentation, campaigns, and automation. PloverCRM is focused on helping teams work new leads before they go cold with source tracking, owner, stage, reminder, history, next action, and mobile follow-up.

## Key comparison points

- Lead follow-up CRM vs email marketing automation CRM
- Individual lead workflow vs contact lists/campaigns
- Reminders and ownership vs sequences/tags/campaigns
- Source tracking and outcome visibility
- WordPress ecosystem fit
- Mobile follow-up differentiation to verify and emphasize
