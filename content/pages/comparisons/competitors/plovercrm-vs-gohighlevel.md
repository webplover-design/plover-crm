## Strategic job

Capture GoHighLevel comparison intent while keeping PloverCRM positioned as lighter, WordPress-first, and focused on lead control after capture.

## Core angle

GoHighLevel is an all-in-one agency and marketing platform with funnels, automation, messaging, CRM, and client-management capabilities. PloverCRM is a focused WordPress-first lead CRM for businesses that want to manage website leads, ad leads, webhooks, source tracking, reminders, stages, mobile follow-up, and outcomes without adopting a broad agency platform.

## Key comparison points

- All-in-one agency platform vs focused WordPress lead CRM
- Funnel/marketing automation depth vs simple lead-control setup
- Client subaccounts and agency features vs WordPress website lead workflow
- Setup complexity and adoption
- Mobile access and follow-up
- Pricing model, verified before publishing
