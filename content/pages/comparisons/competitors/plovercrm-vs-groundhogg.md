## Strategic job

Show when a simpler lead-control CRM is a better fit than a deeper WordPress marketing automation platform.

## Core angle

Groundhogg is a WordPress CRM and marketing automation platform built around funnels, email, segmentation, and automation. PloverCRM is focused on controlling inbound leads after capture: source, owner, stage, reminder, history, next action, mobile access, and outcomes.

## Key comparison points

- Marketing automation/funnels vs lead follow-up operations
- Automation depth vs setup simplicity
- Contact nurturing vs active lead management
- WordPress-native workflow
- Mobile follow-up differentiation to verify
