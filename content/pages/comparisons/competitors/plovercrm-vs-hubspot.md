## Strategic job

Compare PloverCRM with a widely known SaaS CRM without positioning PloverCRM as a universal HubSpot replacement.

## Core angle

HubSpot is a broad CRM, marketing, sales, and service platform. PloverCRM is a focused WordPress-first lead CRM for businesses that mainly need to control what happens after website forms, ad leads, webhooks, and manual inquiries come in.

## Key comparison points

- Broad SaaS CRM platform vs focused WordPress lead CRM
- Marketing/sales/service suite vs capture-to-follow-up workflow
- External CRM setup vs WordPress-first starting point
- Per-seat/platform cost considerations, verified before publishing
- Source tracking, reminders, stages, history, mobile follow-up
- When HubSpot is the better fit
