## Strategic job

Compare PloverCRM with the closest broad WordPress-native CRM competitor.

## Core angle

Jetpack CRM is a broad WordPress CRM with customer-management, client, quote, invoice, and transaction-style capabilities. PloverCRM is more focused on turning WordPress form submissions, ad leads, webhooks, and manual inquiries into follow-up-ready lead records.

## Key comparison points

- Broad WordPress CRM vs focused WordPress lead-control CRM
- Customer/client management vs new lead follow-up
- Quotes/invoices/transactions vs source/reminder/stage/next action
- WordPress-native credibility
- Setup complexity and time to first lead workflow
- Mobile follow-up as a major PloverCRM differentiator to verify and emphasize
