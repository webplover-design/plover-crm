## Strategic job

Compare PloverCRM with the closest adjacent job-to-be-done competitor: mobile-first lead follow-up.

## Core angle

Privyr is a mobile-first lead follow-up CRM for salespeople and teams handling leads from ads, forms, and other channels. PloverCRM is WordPress-first lead control that turns website forms, ad leads, webhooks, and manual inquiries into structured CRM records with source, history, reminders, next steps, and mobile follow-up.

## Key comparison points

- WordPress-first lead CRM vs mobile-first sales follow-up CRM
- Website/form ecosystem fit
- Ad lead handling
- WhatsApp/call/SMS follow-up capabilities to verify
- CRM record depth: source, history, reminders, stages, next actions
- Mobile experience and notifications
- Setup path for WordPress businesses
