## Strategic job

Explain the difference between a focused WordPress lead-control CRM and a broader WordPress ERP/business management suite.

## Core angle

WP ERP includes CRM as part of a larger WordPress business suite with HR/accounting-style modules. PloverCRM is purpose-built for lead-based businesses that need to capture, track, assign, remind, follow up, and understand outcomes after a lead comes in.

## Key comparison points

- Focused lead CRM vs broad ERP/business suite
- Lead follow-up workflow vs wider business operations
- Setup complexity
- Team adoption
- Mobile lead follow-up differentiation to verify
