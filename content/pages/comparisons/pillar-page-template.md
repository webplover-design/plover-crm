## Strategic job

Explain a broader buying decision in a way that helps readers choose the right category of tool, not just PloverCRM.

## Required sections

1. Hero / decision framing
2. Quick answer
3. Comparison table
4. Category definitions
5. Workflow comparison
6. Pricing/setup model comparison where relevant
7. Mobile access and follow-up section where relevant
8. Where each option is better
9. How PloverCRM fits
10. Decision checklist
11. FAQ
12. Final CTA

## Editorial rules

- Stay WordPress-first.
- Sell lead control after capture.
- Explain tradeoffs honestly.
- Do not make unsupported feature, pricing, or performance claims.
- Use direct answer blocks for SEO/AEO.
- Use tables to improve clarity, not to force fake wins.
