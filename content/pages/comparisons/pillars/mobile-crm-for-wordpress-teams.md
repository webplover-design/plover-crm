## Strategic job

Own the mobile-access differentiator by explaining why WordPress lead follow-up should not be trapped inside the WordPress admin dashboard.

## Core message

A WordPress CRM should help your team respond when the lead is still warm. PloverCRM gives teams web and mobile access so they can open lead details, see source/history, update stages, set reminders, and follow up from desktop, iPhone, or Android.

## Comparison angles

- WordPress admin-only CRM vs web/mobile CRM
- Mobile-responsive dashboard vs native mobile app
- New-lead notifications
- Opening lead details from mobile
- Calling, emailing, messaging, or updating status from mobile
- Setting reminders away from the desk
- Keeping desktop and mobile records synced
- Speed-to-lead and field-team use cases
