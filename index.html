PloverCRM is planned around common WordPress form workflows such as Contact Form 7, Fluent Forms, Elementor Forms, WPForms, Gravity Forms, and other supported sources.

No. PloverCRM is designed to work with your existing lead sources instead of forcing you to replace everything.
