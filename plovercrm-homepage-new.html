FAQ

Questions before you download.

Practical answers about setup, sources, integrations, and follow-up workflows.

Setup and trial
Is there a 30-day free trial?

Yes. You can download the plugin as a 30-day trial after signup.

Do I need a credit card?

No credit card is required to start.

How long does setup take?

The onboarding setup wizard helps you connect your first source in around 2–3 minutes.

Can I try the live demo before installing?

Yes. You can try the live demo with sample data before connecting your own website.

Sources and integrations
Does PloverCRM work with WordPress?

Yes. PloverCRM is built for WordPress-first lead workflows.

Which form plugins are supported?

PloverCRM is planned around common WordPress form workflows such as Contact Form 7, Fluent Forms, Elementor Forms, WPForms, Gravity Forms, and other supported sources.

Can I connect Google Ads and Meta Lead Ads?

Yes. PloverCRM is designed to support lead source tracking and outcome visibility for ad-driven workflows.

Does PloverCRM support webhooks?

Yes. Webhooks are part of the planned lead-source and automation workflow.

Workflow and follow-up
Do I need to replace my existing forms?

No. PloverCRM is designed to work with your existing lead sources instead of forcing you to replace everything.

Do I need to migrate spreadsheets?

No spreadsheet migration is required to begin. You can start with new incoming leads and build from there.

Does PloverCRM have mobile apps?

Yes. PloverCRM supports lead access from desktop, iPhone, and Android.

Can I set follow-up reminders?

Yes. Follow-up reminders help keep next actions visible before the lead gets cold.
