FAQ

Questions before you start.

Clear answers for visitors who understand the problem and need confidence before trying the product.

Does PloverCRM work with WordPress?

Yes. PloverCRM is WordPress-first and supports leads from WordPress forms, plugins, webhooks, and ad sources.

Is this only for Fluent Forms?

No. Fluent Forms is one supported source, but PloverCRM can capture from Contact Form 7, Elementor Forms, ads, webhooks, and other sources.

Can my team use it on mobile?

Yes. PloverCRM supports web and mobile workflows so teams can manage leads from anywhere.

What is the trial offer?

The main offer is a one-month free trial. Advocate program rewards can extend access when users share or write about PloverCRM.
