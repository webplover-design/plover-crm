PloverCRM Homepage Strategy

Landing page flow designed to turn lead chaos into lead control.

This visual plan maps every homepage section to the visitor’s awareness stage, conversion purpose, key content, and the reason it should exist.

Core positioning

PloverCRM should not lead as a generic CRM. It should own the specific problem after capture: WordPress leads are coming in, but follow-up, ownership, source tracking, and pipeline control are scattered.

  • Primary audience: problem-aware visitors
  • Main message: stop losing leads after form submission
  • Primary promise: capture, track, assign, and follow up from one WordPress-based CRM

Conversion journey

The page should move visitors from pain recognition to trust and action.

  • Pain: leads are scattered after capture
  • System: PloverCRM creates one clear workflow
  • Proof: features, benefits, use cases, reviews
  • Action: start trial, compare plans, or book demo
